About IntPub

WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

IntPub is a tool researchers, academics, and scientists can use to self-publish their works on uncensorable & decentralized networks. The protocol is a a scholarship-oriented bridge between existing technologies.

OUR MISSION

IntPub's mission is to make scientific publishing accessible to all, and to make scientific knowledge free to access. We believe that the rights to exploration, research, and publication are fundamental.

OUR VISION

We envision a future where the pipeline of science is transparent from idea, to funding, to experimentation and publication. By making IntPub the base infrastructure layer for science, we believe this is possible.
